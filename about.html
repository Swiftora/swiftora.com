Built for secondhand sellers

Swiftora is an AI-powered toolkit for vintage and resale sellers. It turns a few details and photos into a platform-ready listing, suggests price ranges based on comparables, and shows your net profit after fees and shipping.

What Swiftora helps you do

  • List faster: draft titles and descriptions from a quick prompt.
  • Price smarter: guidance tuned for vintage, collectibles, and oddities.
  • Track ROI: see cost, fees, shipping, and net profit at a glance.

Why we’re building this

Most listing tools are made for brand-new retail products. Swiftora is purpose-built for secondhand items—where condition, era, and quirks matter. Our goal is to remove busywork so you can spend more time sourcing and selling.

Roadmap (high level)

  • Image understanding to auto-extract attributes
  • Comps and pricing confidence from sold data
  • Multi-platform listing exports
  • Lightweight analytics and ROI tracking
