Photo tips for better matches
  • Use good lighting (near a window or daylight bulb).
  • Shoot on a neutral background (plain table, sheet, or wall).
  • Include multiple angles: front, back, base/mark, and a close-up of texture.
  • Add a size reference (ruler, coin) for scale.
  • Avoid heavy filters; keep colors accurate.
  • Remove clutter so the item stands out.
